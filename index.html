Become a Volunteer

Why Volunteer With Us?

As a volunteer with HandsUnited, you'll have the opportunity to:

  • Make a tangible difference in people's lives
  • Develop new skills and gain valuable experience
  • Meet like-minded individuals and build connections
  • Participate in a variety of projects based on your interests and skills
  • Receive proper training and support throughout your volunteer journey

We have both local and international volunteer opportunities available, ranging from one-day events to long-term commitments.

Where Your Money Goes

We're committed to transparency and responsible stewardship of donations:

  • 75% directly funds our programs and services
  • 15% supports administrative costs
  • 10% goes toward fundraising efforts

All donations are tax-deductible. We provide financial reports and updates to our donors to show the impact of their contributions.
